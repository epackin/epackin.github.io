Alaska Session 1: Agriculture of Denali

Led by Denali Educational Center Field Instructor

Learn about the ecology, history, and diverse wildlife that make Denali one of the most beautiful national parks in the US.

Alaska Session 2: A Native's Alaska

Led by Native Alaskan Eskimo Teacher

Engage in conversations and activities focused on the Alaska Native perspectives of the social, economic, and political influences that affect the people of Alaska.

Alaska Session 3: Aurora Hunting

Led by Alaskan Wildlife Guide

Discover the science and mythology behind the northern lights while gaining insider tips into how to see them and how to capture quality pictures when you do.

Japan Session 1: Origami Workshop

Led by Origami Expert

Lighten your heart during these dark times as you learn the simple yet intricate ancient art of paper folding.

Japan Session 2: Rajio Taisō, Aikido, and Japanese Martial Arts

Led by Expert Takuto

Get moving in this session as you learn and practice Aikido, a traditional Japanese Martial Art that is great for all ages. Warmup will begin with Rajio Taisō, or radio calisthenics, that have been broadcast daily in Japan for nearly 70 years.

Japan Session 3: Calligraphy Workshop

Led by Calligraphy Expert

Receive thorough instruction on calligraphy letterforms, and learn the contemplative and meditative aspects of calligraphy in the process.

Spain Session 1: Tapas Cooking Class

Led by Local Barcelona Chef

Cook alongside a local Spanish chef as you’re guided through this quintessential Spanish tapas menu, a progressive meal that’s meant to be enjoyed slowly and in good company.

Spain Session 2: Catalonia, an Unfinished Story

Led by Catalonia History Professor

Follow the key events in the history of Catalonia and see how history is still unfolding in this cultural epicenter of Spain's Barcelona.

Spain Session 3: The Makings of a Spanish Style Home

Led by Local Spanish Architect

Discover what makes Spanish homes unique and see how some of Barcelonas most famous homes are pushing the envelop in the 21st century.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this all about?

The Lifer Club is an innovative approach to building a community that connects interesting people with diverse backgrounds and life experiences to enjoy and learn from experts, guides, each other, and the broader world around them.

Our exclusive focus is to support a fulfilling and engaging lifestyle for every member no matter what their passions are or where they live, and we wholeheartedly believe that the best kind of experience is life experience.

Who is the ideal participant?

A “Lifer” has the flexibility to prioritize making the most of life. Lifers are lifelong learners, ready to explore their passions, interests, and the broader world around them. They are independent thinkers that willingly contribute to, and actively participate in, the communities of which they are a part.

How much does a course cost?

While the price for each course varies depending on the number of classes, class topics, and travel pack items included, typical courses range from $295-$695 per household for 3 to 8 classes.

This total price includes the courses Travel Pack as well as tips for all instructors.
